How It Works

Simulate · Analyse · Launch with Confidence

SignalTwin transforms marketing decision making from spend-and-learn to a predictive simulate-then-spend approach using AI-powered digital twin technology.

01

Input & Funnel Creation

Businesses provide campaign inputs — ad copy, landing pages, audience targeting. SignalTwin converts this into a structured Funnel Twin model for accurate simulation.

02

Simulation Engine

The platform runs thousands of synthetic customer journeys using AI to simulate user interactions, identifying drop-offs and potential failure points before launch.

03

AI Risk & Performance Analysis

Multi-agent AI (MACE) evaluates audience intent, creative quality, and funnel performance to generate a predictive SignalScore and actionable risk insights.

04

Actionable Strategy Output

SignalTwin provides clear recommendations on what to fix before launch, enabling businesses to optimise campaigns and confidently invest budget.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is SignalTwin and how does it work?
+

SignalTwin is an AI-powered Pre-Spend Digital Twin platform that simulates marketing campaign outcomes before any budget is committed. It builds a digital twin of the marketing funnel, runs thousands of synthetic customer journeys, and outputs a predictive SignalScore with actionable recommendations.

How does the SignalTwin simulation work?
+

The platform creates a digital twin of the marketing funnel and runs thousands of simulated customer interactions using AI models. It identifies friction points, low-intent traffic, and potential conversion issues before launch.

What is the MACE system in SignalTwin?
+

MACE (Multi-Agent Campaign Evaluation) is a system of specialised AI agents that analyse different aspects of a campaign — audience, creative, and performance. These agents combine insights into a single clear score and recommendation.

Who is SignalTwin designed for?
+

SignalTwin is designed for SMEs, marketing teams, and agencies that want to reduce wasted spend and make better decisions without needing advanced data science skills.

What business value does SignalTwin provide?
+

SignalTwin helps businesses reduce acquisition waste, improve conversion rates, and make confident decisions. It transforms marketing from a risky experiment into a predictable and efficient process.
