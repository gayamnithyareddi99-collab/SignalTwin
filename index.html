How SignalTwin Works

SignalTwin transforms marketing decision making from spend and learn to a predictive simulate then spend approach using AI-powered digital twin technology.

01

Input & Funnel Creation

Businesses provide campaign inputs such as ad copy, landing pages, and audience targeting. SignalTwin converts this into a structured Funnel Twin model for analysis.

02

Simulation Engine

The platform runs thousands of synthetic customer journeys using AI to simulate how users interact with the funnel, identifying drop offs and potential failure points.

03

AI Risk & Performance Analysis

Multi-agent AI (MACE) evaluates audience intent, creative quality, and funnel performance to generate a predictive “SignalScore” and risk insights.

04

Actionable Strategy Output

SignalTwin provides clear recommendations on what to fix before launch, enabling businesses to optimise campaigns and confidently invest budget.

Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers about how SignalTwin works, what makes it different, and how it helps businesses reduce risk before spending on marketing.

What is SignalTwin?

SignalTwin is an AI-powered digital twin platform that simulates customer journeys before marketing campaigns are launched. It allows businesses to test strategies in a virtual environment and identify risks before spending real budget.

How is SignalTwin different from traditional analytics tools?

Traditional tools analyse performance after campaigns are launched. SignalTwin uses a "simulate-then-spend" approach, predicting outcomes before any money is spent, helping businesses avoid costly mistakes.

How does SignalTwin simulation work?

The platform creates a digital twin of the marketing funnel and runs thousands of simulated customer interactions using AI models. It identifies friction points, low-intent traffic, and potential conversion issues before launch.

What is the MACE system in SignalTwin?

MACE (Multi-Agent Campaign Evaluation) is a system of AI agents that analyse different aspects of a campaign such as audience, creative, and performance. These agents combine insights into a single clear score and recommendation.

Who is SignalTwin designed for?

SignalTwin is designed for SMEs, marketing teams, and agencies that want to reduce wasted spend and make better decisions without needing advanced data science skills.

What business value does SignalTwin provide?

SignalTwin helps businesses reduce acquisition waste, improve conversion rates, and make confident decisions. It transforms marketing from a risky experiment into a predictable and efficient process.
